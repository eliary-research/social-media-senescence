# snap_quarterly.csv — Data Sources

## Overview

Snapchat (Snap Inc.) quarterly DAU, revenue, and ARPU from IPO (Q1 2017) through Q4 2025.
- **36 quarters** of data (Q1 2017 – Q4 2025)
- DAU = Daily Active Users (millions), as reported in earnings releases
- Revenue = Total quarterly revenue (USD millions)
- ARPU = Average Revenue Per User (USD), calculated as revenue / DAU unless directly reported

## Key Data Notes

1. **DAU definition**: Snap defines DAU as registered users who open the app at least once during the defined 24-hour period. Reported as end-of-quarter average.

2. **2018 DAU decline**: Q2-Q4 2018 saw DAU stagnation/decline (191 → 186M), the only sustained decline in Snap's public history. Attributed to the controversial redesign and Instagram Stories competition.

3. **ARPU calculation**: ARPU = quarterly revenue / average DAU. Snap reports this directly in earnings. Values here are global averages; North America ARPU is significantly higher (~$7-9 in recent quarters).

4. **Q2 2020 revenue dip**: Revenue declined QoQ ($462M → $454M) due to COVID-19 ad spending pullback, despite DAU growth.

5. **2022-2023 revenue pressure**: Revenue growth stalled in 2022-2023 due to Apple's ATT (App Tracking Transparency) impact on ad targeting and macroeconomic headwinds.

6. **Q4 2025 DAU**: First QoQ DAU decline since 2018 (477M → 474M), though still up 5% YoY.

7. **Revenue figures**: Some quarterly revenue values are rounded to one decimal place from official filings. Exact figures can be verified in SEC 10-Q filings.

## Data Quality Assessment

- **High confidence**: DAU figures (directly reported, widely cited, cross-verified)
- **High confidence**: Revenue figures (SEC-filed, audited)
- **Medium-high confidence**: ARPU (calculated from DAU and revenue; may differ slightly from Snap's reported ARPU due to their use of "average" DAU vs end-of-quarter DAU)
